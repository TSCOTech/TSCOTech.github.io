FAQs

Is this service free?

Signing up for an account is free, but there will be a match fee ($10) and refundable deposit ($20) charged for every successful meetup.

How does your service work?

First, sign up for an account with us. Next, sit back, relax, and wait for us to onboard you. After you are onboarded, we will start sending you matches! When you receive a profile, reply with “yes” or “no” (or however else you want to reply) and we will take it from there.

What is a successful match?

A successful match happens when both parties say yes to a meetup, the details are sent and payment is made to confirm the meetup/date.

Why is there a deposit charged?

The deposit is to ensure that both parties turn up and comply with the guidelines. The guidelines are to ensure that the date goes smoothly. It will be refunded if none of the guidelines are broken.

What are the guidelines?

- Changes in time/date within 24 hours before the meetup would not be made
- No harassing of your date
- Being late for more than 15 minutes

What if I become unavailable?

We understand that due to unforeseen circumstances, you may be unable to make it to the meetup. Please inform us at least > 48 hours before the meetup.

What if my date doesn’t show up?

If you have been waiting for more than 15 minutes, you have the option of leaving the meetup location or staying on to see if your date will arrive soon. You may reach out to us for help, however, we may not be able to respond to you in time. So, please be early for your meetup!

How can I update my profile?

You may tell us which details you would like to change over WhatsApp and we will update it for you.
